# Module 4: Applications & Future

Welcome to Module 4 of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. This module examines real-world applications of humanoid robotics and explores future directions in the field.

## Overview

Module 4 brings together all the concepts from previous modules to explore how humanoid robots are applied in real-world scenarios. You'll learn about current applications, ethical considerations, and the future trajectory of humanoid robotics technology.

## What You'll Learn

- How humanoid robots are used in various applications today
- The ethical implications of humanoid robotics in society
- Future directions and emerging trends in the field
- Societal impacts of humanoid robotics technology

## Module Structure

This module contains the following chapters:
1. Applications and Use Cases - Real-world applications of humanoid robots
2. Ethics and Society - Ethical considerations and societal impacts
3. Future Directions and Conclusions - Emerging trends and the future of the field

## Learning Objectives

By completing this module, you will understand:
- Current and emerging applications of humanoid robotics
- The ethical considerations in humanoid robot development and deployment
- How humanoid robotics will evolve in the coming years
- The broader societal implications of advanced robotics technologies
